About Us

Mission Statement: givebackRx empowers you to improve your health and giveback to your community and causes close to your heart.

Heading

Everyone wants their prescription medication at an affordable price. givebackRx is committed to finding the best options, at the best prices, for the medicines you need. We are also deeply passionate about the importance of givingback. That is why, for every purchase made through givebackRx, we donate a portion of the purchase price to the charity of your choice.

Who we are

givebackRx is dedicated to making the world a better place. Many pharmacy discount cards retain their profits for private stakeholders: givebackRx is different. By linking the givebackRx card to a community of charitable organizations, givebackRx looks to share profit with a purpose. In giving you access to affordable prescriptions while connecting you to causes close to your heart, givebackRx puts staying healthy, saving money and givingback all in one place.

How givebackRx works

We provide prescription discounts at over 65,000 pharmacies nationwide, including Walgreens, CVS, RiteAid, and Target.

To get started, simply go to the givebackRx homepageand follow these easy steps:

  1. Type your prescription name into the prescription search bar
  2. Select your coupon
  3. Present it at your pharmacy for savings

To save even more, you can sign up for givebackRx Silver or Gold membership card to use every time you purchase medications. With membership cards, you can select the charity you want to help, and we make a donation to them in your name.

To get started, go to www.givebackrx.com and follow these easy steps:

  1. Click “SIGN UP” in the upper right
  2. Enter your email or phone number
  3. Select your favorite charity
  4. Present your virtual givebackRx card at your pharmacy

Saving money and givingback is as simple as that.
