about us

our mission

givebackRx empowers you to improve your health and giveback to you community and causes close to your heart.

our vision

givebackRx is dedicated to making the world a better place. Many pharmacy discount cards retain their profits for private stakeholders: givebackRx is different. By linking the givebackRx card to a community of charitable organizations, we look to share profit with a purpose. In giving you access to affordable prescriptions while connecting you to causes close to your heart, givebackRx puts staying healthy, saving money and givingback all in one place.
